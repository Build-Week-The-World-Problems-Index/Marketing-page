About

World Problems Index aims to solve the problem of average people not having a big enough voice on the global stages. Our app is designed so that one person, with one voice can become an agent of change by rallying thousands behind their cause. Click the button below to visit our site and become a global agent for change.

Our Mission

To investigate world issues, rally behind a solution and use technology to solves problems around the world. We use use the power of numbers to amplify our voice and get issues resolved.

Future Goals

World Problems Index has been a leader in making change around the world since 1999. We plan to extend our operations into every corner of the planet, using technology to connect humans across the globe to become agents of change.

Better Planet

The World Problems Index is committed to solving world problems. We employ the best investigators, researchers, and planners in the world to not only find the cause of the problem, but to research and implement a solution as well.
